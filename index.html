Offline Mode

Built for patchy safari signal

After the first successful load, this app caches the page, scripts, styles, manifest, icons, and animal photos on your device. Your sightings and bingo card are saved in local storage, so the game still opens and remembers progress without an internet connection.

Photos are sourced from Wikimedia Commons. Each animal card links to its source page for creator and license details.
